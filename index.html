Our Story
Katie and Jacob met through mutual friends in marching band in 2016. Even after meeting, they were slow to become acquaintances. Eventually, Katie started coming to be a part of Jacob's weekly Bible study.

Over the next few months, Katie and Jacob began to hang out more regularly. Although Jacob had feelings for Katie, she did not feel the same way. That, however, did not stop their friendship.

Eventually, conversations about dating resurfaced. They decided to take a leap of faith and go for it, and boy did it pay off!
