Our Story
Katie and Jacob met through mutual friends in marching band in 2016. Even after meeting, they were slow to become acquaintances. Eventually, Katie started coming to be a part of Jacob's weekly Bible study.

Over the next few months, Katie and Jacob began to hang out more regularly. Although Jacob had feelings for Katie, she did not feel the same way. That, however, did not stop their friendship.

Eventually, conversations about dating resurfaced. They decided to take a leap of faith and go for it, and boy did it pay off! The past few years have been an absolute blast, and we can't wait to see what God has in our future together!

Things to Do
Are you looking to make plans during the rest of the weekend? If so, we got you covered!
Auburn University
Auburn is a mere hour away from Newnan. This is, of course, the college Katie and Jacob went to for undergrad.
Chewacla State Park
While you're in Auburn, be sure to check out Chewacla! There are many great trails to hike!
Centennial Olympic Park
If you're flying into Atlanta on your way to town, take a stroll through Olympic Park before leaving town! It's a beautiful green space, and has the GA Aquarium, World of Coca Cola, and much more all within walking distance!
Historic Downtown Newnan
If you need dinner plans, want to go out for drinks, or are just interested in seeing some awesome old buildings, go take a stroll downtown!
